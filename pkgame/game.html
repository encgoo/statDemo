Statistics and Poker Game

This is a simple poker game in javascript. Here all 5 of the dealer's cards and 4 (of 5) of the player's cards are shown. Who is going to win depends on the last card. It is all above STATISTICS. See if you can do better than the computer!
  • Make your prediction by clicking the "User Will Win" button or the "Dealer Will Win" button.
  • Then the app is going to do its prediction as well according to statistics, and updates the table below.
  • Click "Show Last Card", and then the last card will be shown. You can then see whether you have made a better prediction than the app or not.
  • Click "Start New Game" to try a new one.
